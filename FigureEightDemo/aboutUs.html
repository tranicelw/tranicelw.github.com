Figure Eight Enterprise

Figure Eight Enterprise is a full service entertainment company providing professional equipment and personnel to make your next occasion memorable.

We have Entertainment Specialists that will assist you in planning the unique event you imagine. Be it corporate or casual, birthdays or Bar Mitzvahs, Figure Eight is the entertainment company for you.

Figure Eight Enterprise is based out of Mobile, AL and we offer many different entertainment acts. The band that our company is based off of, Figure 8, is noted for its fast paced, high energy show. This fun loving band is full of energy and enthusiasm. Our company came from this concept to deliver unique entertainment experiences.

Our Mission

Provide exciting entertainment that appeals to all ages, with high levels of service, showmanship, enthusiasm and professionalism.

We believe in total entertainment and we are dedicated to customer service. (Besides playing all of your favorite music)

Our Customers

Our customers have one thing in common and that is they want to hire the best! We do weddings, parties, conventions, casinos, colleges, private clubs, wherever you need entertainment call us.
