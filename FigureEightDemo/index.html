Figure 8

FIGURE 8 is noted for its fast paced and high energy show. This fun loving band is full of energy and enthusiasm. Crowds flock to the stage to watch the quick stepping choreography. Audience participation is a natural for this band.

Figure 8 is a show and dance band based out of Mobile, AL. The song list ranges from the 50's to the 90's from Sam Cooke to Garth Brooks and from the Supremes to the Pointer Sisters.

Figure 8 has a song list to satisfy audiences of all ages. A tight rhythm section and hard driving vocals are what makes the group a southeast favorite. Variety music, costume changes, and choreography are one of our trademarks.

Figure 8 comes self contained with sound/light and will work with all clients to make their venue a success.

We are...

Available for weddings, parties, conventions, and universities; our recent engagements include LSU, Mississippi State University, the Isle of Capri Casino, and the Florida Yacht Club.

We offer...

We also have three other bands available for booking. Four Trax is an '80s show band, singing everything from Motown to Elvis. Darlawan Duet performs a wide range of musical styles in full costume. Their performance list includes everything from Temptations to Tricia Yearwood. Ovation offers big-band music, from swing to jazz. We also have karaoke and DJ's including sound and light shows.

Don't hesitate to call on Figure 8 for an evening you won't forget!
