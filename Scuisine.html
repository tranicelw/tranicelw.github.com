Spanish Cuisine

Welcome to the hub of Spanish cuisine. This page is about everything Spanish cuisine. Here, we will go into a small synopsis about Spanish cuisine.

Spanish cuisine can be found all over the world. This type of cuisine consists of ingrediants such as grains, vegatables, spices, meats and oils. The most important ingrediants that can be found in many of Spanish dishs are spices, olive oil and pork. There are many popular dishes that are associated with Spanish cuisine which includes: Paella, Gazpacho, and Spanish Tortilla.
