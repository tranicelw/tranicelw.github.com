Spain

Spain was once a super power and controlled a large portion of the world. Because of this, the Spanish culture and language has influenced many countries around the world.

It is the reason that the majority of South and Central America are practice Catholicism. It is also the reason that most of the people from South America up to the United States speak Spanish or another dialect that has a strong relationship with the Spanish language.

The influence of this once great country cannot be denied and the Spanish language is a very useful language to know, especially if a person wants to be marketable in North America.
