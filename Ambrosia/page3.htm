AD Specifications

Ambrosia permits banners to be displayed along the bottom of the page. Only Standard and flash text banner ads are permitted. Ad must be no larger than 480x60 and the maximum file size is 20kb. File must be saved in a jpeg or gif format. Ad themes and color schemes should be within good taste, refined, and presented in ways which correspond with the poise of Ambrosia Bakery. Ads containing sound are not permitted. Ambrosia reserves the right to decline any company’s advertising based on any reason.

Franchisor Obligation

Ambrosia wants to ensure that you are successful by providing distinctive training throughout the duration of the franchise. To create cohesion and unity franchisees will be equipped with knowledge that warrants operational efficiencies, quality product production, and superior customer service. We offer 24 hour support through webinars and our leadership hotline for any assistance that may be needed. Ambrosia knows the importance of location, that’s why we provide designated location suggestions to help you find a location that is both affordable and profitable. Ambrosia has licensed professionals to train all new hires to improve manager and employee retention. Customer service workshops and team meetings will be conducted daily to instill Ambrosia’s values and culture.

Marketing Strategies

In addition to supplying you with all the necessary aids to ensure your success, all marketing research is handled for you by headquarters. Sales and promotional tools to help you promote your new location will be available on a monthly basis.

Franchisee Obligations

It is the responsibility of the franchisee and their employees to adhere to all company policies and procedures. Monthly inspections will be performed to ensure all franchisee locations are within compliance of safety and health regulations. Ambrosia expects all franchisee to uphold to a high standard of quality, while exceeding customer expectations.
