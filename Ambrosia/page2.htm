Marketing Plan

Ambrosia is a bakery that is native to Pensacola, Florida. Established in the summer of 2009, the dream began when two college students Ta’Quanyia and Tranice wanted to share their cupcake recipes with the local community. Soon that dream would turn into a reality and Ambrosia was created. Some years later Ambrosia has become a household name for providing beautifully hand crafted signature cupcakes for sweet eaters all across the United States.


MISSION STATEMENT

"Ambrosia strives to provide customers with freshly baked, beautifully hand crafted gourmet cupcakes. Ambrosia’s signature flavors and styles provide a unique cupcake indulging experience."


GOALS

  • Ambrosia wishes to provide superior customer service while creating a unique bakery experience
  • Ambrosia wants to continually expand into international markets and become a household name for signature cupcakes globally
  • Ambrosia wants to maintain great consumer expectations and brand perception
  • Ambrosia wants to retain quality employees that are committed to the brand and vision
  • Ambrosia wants to operate with best practices to maintain optimal levels of corporate social responsibility
