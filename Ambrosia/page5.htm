Marketing Plan

Market Industry

Market

Upon analyzing the market data the Coffee/Snack Industry is continually growing each year. This industry shows an annual growth rate of 2.7% within the last five years. For new entrants that plan to enter this industry, the trend for growth is about 1.4%. Over the next five years the annual growth rate is expected to be at 3.8 The Coffee/Snack Industry is very lucrative with revenues totaling 30.2 billion dollars, and a current profit of 1.8 billion dollars. Although this industry is very profitable there is intense competition. The two key competitors in this industry are Starbucks and Dunkin’ Donuts. Presently, Starbucks and Dunkin Donuts account for more than 60% of the industry’s market share, leaving the remaining 40% to small business operators.

Product

The Coffee/Snack industry provides various products and services dealing with snack foods. Ambrosia fits in the snack shops segment of this industry. Our business model is to provide beautifully hand crafted gourmet cupcakes, while offering quality customer service.

Consumer

The age groups that are targeted within the Coffee/Snack industry are young adults 18-35 year olds and the baby boomers 50+ years old. Young adults tend to spend most of their discretionary income eating out. Baby-boomers tend to have more discretionary income which means they are more likely to spend money on eating out. Even though this industry has specified targets anyone of any age can enjoy Ambrosia’s world famous cupcakes.

Marketing Strategies

Corporate Identity

Ambrosia Bakery represents originality at its finest by providing our signature flavors and unique styles in cupcakes; it makes it hard for our consumers to resist our products. Ambrosia Bakery is the appropriate name for our franchise and is to be used in all publications, advertising, and signage. “The Ambrosia Experience,” is a phrase used to describe the overall atmosphere that is embraced at our locations. This phrase may only be used with authorized consent. Our logo design and color schemes are not to be altered in any way. For additional details please refer to our Corporate Identity Manual for specifications and regulations at www.ambrosiabakery.com

Public Relations

All public relation affairs will be conducted by our corporate office in Pensacola, Florida. Event updates and new product offerings will be announced via our website and monthly newsletters. Quarterly charity events will be sponsored on a selective basis. For additional information please refer to our charitable contributions brochure.

Trade Shows

Ambrosia Bakery is an active member of the Retail Bakers of America (retailbakersofamerica.org), in which we compete in yearly bake-off competitions and create a one of a kind cupcake masterpiece. Ambrosia is also listed as a partner for SkillsUSA (skillsusa.org), a commercial baking competition where we offer the postsecondary winner the opportunity to join the Ambrosia team.

Direct Mail

Direct Mail will be used to mail a monthly newsletter to franchise owners share corporate updates and local events within the community. Consumers on our direct mailing list will receive monthly coupons and birthday gift subscriptions.

Sales Promotion and Advertising

Ambrosia Bakery’s advertisement and promotions will be directed by each individual franchise, with suggestions tools and final approval being made by the Marketing and Advertising department at headquarters. Ambrosia will use various methods to reach our desired target market such as radio ads in Spotify and iHeartRadio, ads on YouTube, and other various social media sites such as Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and Pintrest. Advertising and promotional budgets are based on the population size of each geographical location.
